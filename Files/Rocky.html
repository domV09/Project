Description

Rocky Balboa, club fighter from the mean streets of Philadelphia, gets an unlikely shot at the
heavyweight championship of the world by taking on champion Apollo Creed.
The irresistible story of the boxer who gives his all to win the championship title won Oscars
for Best Picture and Best Director and catapulted Sylvester Stallone to superstardom.
